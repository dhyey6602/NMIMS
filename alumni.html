Benefits of Joining our Alumni Network

The Alumni Portal allows our graduates to get connected with peers across industries, create special interest groups, stay abreast of happenings at the university, and leverage the network to further their professional and personal interests.

Knowledge Sharing

Our alumni portal allows you to connect with your peers by profession, batch, or region. You can share news, blogs, experiences, and job opportunities with your batch mates and find other alumni to connect with.

Networking

Our alumni are spread across the world. Our platform enables them to stay connected with their alma mater, peers and batchmates and be updated with the latest happenings & events.

Job Postings

In order to support the career growth of our alumni, we regularly share job openings from our hiring partners. The alumni network also provides the advantage of gaining references and opportunities from fellow alumni.
