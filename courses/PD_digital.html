Program Features

Highlights:
  • The Professional Diploma in Digital Marketing (in partnership with Stukent) program is ideal for professionals looking to acquire new skill sets or fresher’s wanting to boost their resume
  • The course is also an ideal beginning for anyone interested in understanding the nuances of Digital Marketing
  • It helps recognise emerging trends and technologies like Programmatic Ad Buying, Chatbot, Digital Wallets and Marketing Automation
  • It will also make you explore tools like Google AdWords, Google Analytics, Facebook and Instagram Advertising, YouTube and Video Marketing, ORM & CRM tools etc.
  • It will help:
    1)Learn the principles of Digital Marketing
    2)Optimise search advertising campaigns, with a special focus on Google AdWords for end-to-end campaign management
    3)Build effective strategies across social media platforms (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and LinkedIn) to engage new and existing audiences whilst achieving business goals
    4)Implement marketing fundamentals in the digital world
Outcomes:
  • Effectively devise an integrated & cost-effective digital marketing plan.
  • Effectively monitor, measure and adapt the marketing plan for continuous improvement.
