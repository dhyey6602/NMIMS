Program Features

Highlights:
  • The one-year program provides a specialised curriculum which is based on industry insights and is focussed on the Asian market
  • Experienced faculty, who are a mix of academia and industry (IIM, IIT) will provide aspiring wealth managers with a holistic overview.
  • Learn to implement an innovative approach acquired through Blended Learning – Hands-on Exposure with live instruction sessions
  • Wealth management professionals will earn a deeper insight into the building blocks of managing wealth through academic exposure.
Outcomes:
  • Acquire professional competencies to pursue a career in professional wealth management
  • Obtain an enhanced understanding of financial goals and wealth planning of clients
  • Ability to innovate financial products and the financial market.
  • Skilfully design investment policy statements and manage portfolios.
  • Effectively apply the quantitative skills to design a successful portfolio
