Program Features

Highlights:
  • Spread across four semesters, the program focuses on teaching the fundamentals of technology to build a dynamic career in the information technology marketplace.
  • The program develops critical thinking, managerial skills &stresses on the use of modern techniques to analyse and develop business solutions in the IT field.
  • The courseware focuses on familiarising participants with the latest technology disrupting the current business scenarios.
  • The program familiarises candidates with various management principles & practices, as well as concepts of organisational behaviour, information technology in management, and system analysis, digital payments, and enterprise resource planning
Outcomes:
  • Evaluate appropriate business strategies, practices, and theories for business decisions
  • Determine appropriate information systems solutions using current and emerging technologies
  • Demonstrate proficiency in understanding and managing the most critical issues of technology investment
  • Manage and adopt new technologies within the changing IT scenario
  • Effectively understand the factors needed to initiate business process reengineering
